Wormpex AI Research is the research branch of BianLiFeng (便利蜂), a fast growing convenient store chain in China backed by a global capital. At Wormpex AI research, we build state-of-the-art AI technologies to facilitate new retail logistics from storefronts, warehouses to manufacture. We explore a variety of research domains, e.g. Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Robotics, Operations Research, Graphics, HCI or a combination of the above, so as to empower the next generation retail business operation and shopping experiences.

Join Us!

Are you passionate about using AI to drive future retail business end-to-end? Do you want to have the freedom to propose, experiment and execute your brilliant ideas to hard yet practical problems? Do you believe in business-driven technology development and fast iteration? If so, Wormpex AI Research is the right place for you to make a huge impact. 

At Wormpex AI Research, we deliver advanced AI technologies to different stage of our retail operation process, and conduct exploratory research to secure future opportunities.

We are looking for experienced research/data scientist at different levels (ranging from entry level to director level), who can craft state-of-the-art solutions to practical industrial problems. The exact position will be crafted commensurate with the candidates’ experiences. The position requires gathering and ingesting large volumes of data, and processing them to create insightful descriptive or 2D/3D visual representations to guide business decisions. Specifically, we are interested in candidates in (but not limited to) the following areas:
